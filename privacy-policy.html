Privacy Policy

Thank you for using the Stones in a Jar Chrome Extension. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we handle user data.

Data Collection

This extension allows users to create "stones" with a title provided by the user. These stones are stored locally in the user's browser and are not transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Data Usage

The data (stones) created by the user is used solely for the purpose of displaying and managing the stones within the extension. The data remains private and accessible only within the user's browser.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any user data to external parties. All data stays within the user's local browser storage.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at javiercampos.es.
